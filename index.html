By Bennett

Hi. My name is Bennett. I started life is meaningful by accident at the University of Michigan — I set up a table, two chairs, and a sign that said tell me the most meaningful moment of your life. One hundred people sat down with me.

What I found stunned me: our most meaningful moments are guides to who we are, what we value, and what our purpose is. But we've never had a space to understand and align with them. I'm building life is meaningful to be that space to help you live a life of consistent meaning.
