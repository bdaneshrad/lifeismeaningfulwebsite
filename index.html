By Bennett

By Bennett Daneshrad

Hi. My name is Bennett. I started Life is Meaningful by accident at the University of Michigan — I sat down with a table, two chairs, and a sign that said 'tell me the most meaningful moment of your life.' One hundred people sat down with me. What I discovered was simple yet changed everything for me: the most meaningful moments of our lives are guides to help us understand who we are, what we value, and what our purpose is. But our society doesn't provide a space to understand and align with our moments. I'm building Life is Meaningful to be that space — a place to help you discover and decode your meaningful moments to find clarity, purpose, and live a life aligned with what's meaningful to you.

Spreading authentic connection and meaning is at the center of what I do. Through Life is Meaningful, I hope to support people in connecting with their meaningful moments and finding clarity in their life direction. If you'd like to work together on a deeper level, feel free to reach out anytime — bennett@lifeismeaningful.org
